About Finch

Name: Finch Auden
Age: 23
Pronouns: He/Him
Alignment: Chaotic Neutral
Hey there! I'm Finch, or Finny. I'm a professionally published author of creative nonfiction. I write a genre called lyric essay or collage essay, and for the most part I write about my personal experiences. I'm also a PhD student studying psychology at a well-known research university.

I'm a transgender neutrois. Other labels that might apply to me are agender and libramasculine. I'm also gray-asexual (somewhere between demisexual and autosexual) and panromantic.

I've been diagnosed autistic & bipolar 1 (though I personally prefer the older term manic depression) & ADHD & OCD. Say that five times fast! The research work I do, not to mention my literary work, is informed by my own neurodiverse perspective. I study and wrire about topics that I have personal insight into.

I'm culturally and ethnically Jewish (Ashkenazi). I was born into an interfaith family. I'm atheist by constitution, and have no belief in spiritual or supernatural phenomena. I'm a secular Buddhist.

I love Nintendo. I'm a total nerd who collects Game Boy and Nintendo DS consoles. Pokémon has been my special interest for over a decade! I'm also into tabletop gaming, including Magic: The Gathering and Pokémon TCG, board games like Catan and Smash Up, and RPGs like D&D.
