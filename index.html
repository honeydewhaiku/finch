Here are some pieces of trivia about me:


I'm autistic & bipolar & ADHD & OCD. Say that five times fast! My research approach is informed by my own neurodiverse perspective, and I tend to study topics that I have personal insight into.

My pronouns are he/him/his. I'm transmasculine and neutrois, and perhaps I would label myself as a demiboy. I'm also gray-asexual, and indeed hopelessly biromantic.

I'm culturally and ethnically Jewish (Ashkenazi). I am atheistic and don't identify with any particular religion, but I might call myself an aspiring Buddhist.

I love Nintendo. I'm a total nerd who collects Game Boy and Nintendo DS consoles. Pokémon has been my special interest for over a decade! I'm also into tabletop gaming, including Magic: The Gathering and Pokémon TCG, board games like Catan and Smash Up, and RPGs like D&D and Pathfinder!
